This is me.

I am located in Fresno, California where I have lived my whole life. It's a big little city in the Central Valley and I love living here. I enjoy attending Fresno Grizzlies games, finding local eateries and bars, and wine tasting at my cousins', and other local wineries.

In my life outside of work and school, I love anything that allows me to be creative. I like to take on everything from big projects like rehabbing furniture, to small projects like creating handmade cards. I love to bake! I'm not anywhere close to being a professional baker, but I enjoy the process of taking the ingredients and creating something. I also love to travel. I lived in China for a year, and was able to visit Thailand while I was there. I've been back to China once since then, with a trip planned for next year.
